I used to like making music a lot:

  • Ouch PLAY

    My favourite. It's a bit of a technical étude.

  • Anti-Eros PLAY

    This one is more experimental; I sampled only mechanical objects.

  • It's Late, The Time PLAY

    I wanted to make a reggae/dub piece. The interlude in the middle is me going to the bank. The bit about cowboys is from a Hank Williams documentary.

  • Soy PLAY

    In retrospect, the bass drum and snare hit pretty hard here. The ending is me making myself some tea.

  • Hands On PLAY

    For this I sampled She's Gone by Hall and Oates. I actually fed the output from my soundcard playing the YouTube video back into itself in a pinch to show a friend how I make music. I ended up liking the result and kept it. Again the harder BD and snare, especially for such a slow tempo, make this sound a bit more dated than I would like.

  • Jean PLAY

    Dolly Parton meets Herbie Hancock.

  • Hotel PLAY

    Serge Gainsbourg sample. There was always something that bugged me about this one but I could never fix it.
